## What happened
On July 2nd, the 2026 World Cup saw three significant 16th-final matchups that advanced teams into the Round of 16. In Inglewood, California, Spain faced Austria, with both teams fielding strong lineups. Later that day, also in Inglewood, Portugal took on Croatia in another highly anticipated clash featuring star players from both sides.

The action continued across the continent in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, where Switzerland played Algeria. These matches were among the final six 16th-final confrontations, with the outcomes directly determining three more of the eight Round of 16 keys. The results from these games, alongside those from July 3rd, completed the full lineup for the next stage of the tournament.
