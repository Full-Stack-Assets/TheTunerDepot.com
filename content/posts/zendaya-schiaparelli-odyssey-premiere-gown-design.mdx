## What happened
Zendaya attended the London premiere of her upcoming film "The Odyssey" wearing a striking ensemble from Schiaparelli's newest Fall/Winter 2026-27 Haute Couture collection. The actress, though not present at the collection's runway debut in Paris, showcased one of its most dramatic pieces.

The gown featured a sculpted, porcelain-white bodice that appeared molded to her form, highlighting the female silhouette with hyper-realistic detail in the front and a corset-style lacing at the back. Complementing this, the skirt was a cascade of white and clear beads, designed to mirror light and flow to the floor, where icy pumps completed the look. Maison Schiaparelli noted that the fringed skirt was "illuminated from within," adding to its ethereal quality. The ensemble was accessorized with a five-strand diamond necklace, stud earrings, and her wedding rings, all styled by Law Roach.
