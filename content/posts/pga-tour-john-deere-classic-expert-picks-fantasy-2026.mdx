## What happened
The PGA TOUR's panel of experts has released their weekly selections and analysis for the John Deere Classic, coinciding with significant updates to the PGA TOUR Fantasy Game for the 2026 season. These enhancements now include in-tournament rostering features, allowing players more dynamic control over their teams. Each expert's lineup comprises four starters, including a captain for bonus points, and two bench players who can be rotated after each round, with a strict limit of three uses per golfer per segment across the season.

Among the notable picks, Senior Manager Will Gray highlighted J.T. Poston for a Top 10 Finish, citing his recent win at the Memorial Tournament and strong performance at Shinnecock Hills. Gray also backed Rickie Fowler for a Top 20 Finish, anticipating a return to form. Chris Breece, Senior Content Manager for Golfbet, favored Pierceson Coody for a Top 20 Finish, noting his consistent top-25 placements this year. Breece also suggested Eric Cole for an End of Round 1 – Top 10, based on his impressive 64-63 scores in recent Thursdays.
